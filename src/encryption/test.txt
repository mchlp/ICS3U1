0. Buttons
2. A button is something you can press, which will send a signal to the computer. Examples of buttons include the power button on a monitor.
Sockets/Ports
A socket/port is an opening you can plug a device with a corresponding plug into. It allows the computer to send or receive data to and from the connected device. An example of a socket/port is a USB port.
Slots
A slot is a long, flat opening which you can insert a disc into. An example of a slot is the optical disk drive slot.
